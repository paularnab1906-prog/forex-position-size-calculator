Overview
This tool helps traders calculate proper position size based on account balance, risk percentage, and stop loss distance.

The calculator supports Forex pairs, JPY pairs, Gold (XAUUSD), and Cryptocurrency pairs.

It is designed to help traders manage risk and avoid over-leveraging.

Features
• Position size calculation
• Risk amount calculation
• Risk / reward ratio
• Spread adjustment
• Forex pair support
• JPY pair support
• Gold (XAUUSD)
• Crypto pairs
• Trade summary export
• Copy lot size function
• Copy trade setup

How To Use

Step 1
Select the trading pair.

Step 2
Enter your account balance.

Step 3
Enter the percentage of account you want to risk.

Step 4
Enter spread in pips if needed.

Step 5
Enter entry price, stop loss, and take profit.

Step 6
Click "Calculate".

The calculator will display:
• Stop loss distance
• Take profit distance
• Risk / reward ratio
• Risk amount
• Recommended lot size
• Potential profit
