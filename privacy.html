ForexPro Calculator Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Information We Collect

ForexPro Calculator is a client-side web application that runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or trading data to our servers.

Local Storage

The application uses browser local storage to save your preferences, settings, and trade journal entries locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Authentication

For demonstration purposes, the application includes mock login/signup functionality. In a real deployment, user authentication would be handled securely through proper server-side systems with encrypted data transmission.

Third-Party Services

This application loads fonts from Google Fonts and uses standard web technologies. No tracking scripts, analytics, or advertising services are included.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, your trading calculations and journal entries remain private and secure on your device. We recommend keeping your browser and device secure to protect your data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the information provided on our contact page.

Use for educational purposes only. Always manage your risk responsibly.
